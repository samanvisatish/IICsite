JSS Science And Technology University

          

            JSS STU (formerly SJCE) aims to create a futuristic global university with a learner-centric ecosystem and a focus on
                science and technology. The University is committed to deliver high quality educational opportunities
                for youth and transform not only its neighbourhood but offer courses to equip aspiring youth to meet the
                global needs of industry in every sector. The Mission is to establish one of the world's best
                universities, which is broad-based, sustainable, value driven and research-intensive and integrates
                nation building.

About us

      

        

          
What Do We Do?

          
The Innovation Council is responsible for conducting various innovation and entrepreneurship-related activities, as well as organizing workshops/seminars/interactions with a multitude of entrepreneurs, investors, professionals, creating a mentor pool for student innovators.

        

        

          
        

      

      

        

          
        

        

          
Mission

          
 To Promote innovation in the Institution through multitudinous modes leading to an innovation promotion eco-system in the campus.
            And to Establish Functional Ecosystem for Scouting Ideas and Pre-incubation of Ideas.
          

        

      

      

        

          
Vision

          

            Develop better Cognitive Ability amongst Technology Students.
            Identify and reward innovations and share success stories.
            Prepare institute for Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements Framework
